## Wiring
For NodeMCU-32S 

### PMS5003
- VCC to VIN (5v)
- GND to GND
- TXD to 26
- SET to 27

### BME280
- VIN to 3.3V
- GND to GND
- SCL to 22
- SDA to 23

### BH1750
- VCC to 3.3V
- GND to GND
- SCL to 22
- SDA to 23


### MH-Z19
 - VIN to VIN (5V)
 - GND to GND
 - TX to pin 18
 - RX to pin 19
 
 ### HC-SR501
 - VIN to VIN (5V)
 - GND to GND
 - OUT to 21
